# Welcome to Resourceful Sheep!

**Resourceful Sheep** is a fully customizable Minecraft mod that lets you raise sheep capable of producing different resources, from simple Cobblestone to the mighty Nether Star!

Whether you are a player looking to automate your resource gathering, or a Modpack creator wanting to design complex crossbreeding trees, this wiki contains everything you need to know.

---

## What does it do ?

* **Resource Production:** Each sheep is linked to a specific resource and can be sheared.
* **Tier System:** Breed sheep to higher tiers to increase their production output.
* **Crossbreeding:** Extract DNA from mobs using Syringes and combine them in the DNA Splicer to create artificial mutations.
* **Highly Configurable:** Everything is data-driven. Change drops, feeding items, mutations, and spawning rules using simple JSON files.
* **JEI Integration:** Full support for *Just Enough Items* to view drops, feeding habits, and DNA crossbreeding recipes directly in-game.
